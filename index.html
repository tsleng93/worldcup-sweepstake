▸ Prize rules (click to expand)
🥇 Winner's prize
The participant who holds the team that wins the tournament — regardless of which rank tier that team was drawn from.
🥈 2nd team prize
The participant whose 2nd-ranked team progresses furthest in the tournament.
🥉 3rd team prize
The participant whose 3rd-ranked team progresses furthest in the tournament.
🏅 4th team prize
The participant whose 4th-ranked team progresses furthest in the tournament.

How "furthest" is decided — tiebreakers applied in order:

  1. 1.The team that reached the later round wins. (e.g. quarter-final exit beats last-16 exit)
  2. 2.If knocked out at the same round: the team eliminated later in that round wins — i.e. losing on penalties beats losing in extra time beats losing in normal time.
  3. 3.If still tied: best goal difference from games played in that round.
  4. 4.If still tied: most goals scored from games played in that round.
  5. 5.If still tied: apply tiebreakers 2–4 again using the stats from the previous round, and repeat backwards through the tournament until resolved.

Note: the 2nd, 3rd and 4th prizes are independent — a participant can win multiple prizes if they hold teams across different rank tiers. For the 6-person draw (8 teams each), each person's teams are still identified by their original rank tier.
